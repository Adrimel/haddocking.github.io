HADDOCK

High Ambiguity Driven biomolecular DOCKing


based on biochemical and/or biophysical information.

  • S.J. de Vries, A.D.J. van Dijk, M. Krzeminski, M. van Dijk, A. Thureau, V. Hsu, T. Wassenaar and A.M.J.J. Bonvin
